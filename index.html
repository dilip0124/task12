Popular Foods

Pizza

Italian origin, loved worldwide for its cheesy, saucy, and crispy taste.

Biryani

Spiced rice dish from India, famous for its aroma and flavors.

Sushi

Japanese delicacy made with vinegared rice and seafood or veggies.

Burger

Classic fast food with a patty, veggies, and sauces in a bun.

Pasta

Italian staple, comes in many shapes and sauces like Alfredo and Arrabbiata.

Ice Cream

Frozen dessert loved by all ages, available in endless flavors.

Fun Food Facts

  • Pizza was invented in Naples, Italy, in the 18th century.
  • Biryani is believed to have been brought to India by Persian travelers.
  • Sushi does not always mean raw fish; it refers to vinegared rice.
  • The world’s largest burger weighed over 1 ton!
  • Ice cream cones were popularized at the 1904 World’s Fair.
